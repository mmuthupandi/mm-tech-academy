## Animation Characteristics

| Card Type | Animation | Speed | Direction | Color |
|-----------|-----------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Hardware | Floating nodes | Slow (8s) | Multi-directional | Green |
| Cybersecurity | Pulsing waves | Medium (3s) | Outward | Green |
| Programming | Scrolling lines | Medium (3-4s) | Upward | Green |
| Data Science | Neural pulse | Medium (4s) | Pulsing | Green |
| Energy | Rotating rays | Slow (12s) | Clockwise | Orange |
| Marketing | Expanding ripples | Medium (3s) | Outward | Blue |
| Photography | Aperture | Medium (4s) | Scale + Rotate | Purple |
| Business | Rising graph | Fast (2s) | Upward diagonal | Green |

## Mobile vs Desktop

### Desktop (>768px)
- Full animation complexity
- Opacity: 8% → 15% on hover
- All keyframes active
- 60fps target

### Mobile (≤768px)
- Simplified animations
- Opacity: 6% → 10% on hover
- Reduced keyframe complexity
- 30-60fps target
- Mascot hidden (Services section)
